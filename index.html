About Paneer Tikka

Panner tikka is a North Indian appetizer where chunks of paneer are marinated in spiced yougurt and grilled in a Tandoor,a traditional clay oven.
The term paneer refers to Indian cottage cheese and tikka translates to chunks of grilled mear,or a vegetrain option,in this case it is the paneer.

All of the magic happens in the tandoor and we do not have that at homes.so for home version we turn on to electric ovens , gas or air fryer to recreate it.
